About Me

My name is Dylan Gibson. I currently work as a paramedic at Butler County Sheriff's Office. I am building my future in IT and cybersecurity. This website is a place for me to share my background, experience, goals, certifications, and the path I am taking as I continue learning and growing in the tech field.

My Background

I have always been a geek at heart. Throughout my teenage years I spent a lot of time learning about computers and everything to do with technology. The last two years of high school I took the Information Technology program at Warren County Career Center. After high school, I started attending college at Sinclair Community College. At sinclair I was majoring in Network Administration. During that time at Sinclair, one of my best friends got me to volunteer at our local fire department. I enjoyed the fire service so much that I decided to change my career path and the rest has been history until now.

My Interest in Cybersecurity

After making the decision to pursue a career in the IT field, I started to look at the different paths that I could take. Since I had already built a strong foundation with Networking and PC hardware, I started looking at the different IT fields that I could learn and grow into. My knowledge has always been limited with all the different types of programming luanguages, so I decided to focus on cybersecurity.

What Drives Me

I value growth, hard work, and learning new skills. My goal is not only to change careers, but to build a strong future through knowledge, persistence, and practical experience. I want my work to reflect both professionalism and purpose.
