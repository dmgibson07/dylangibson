Career History

This page shares the career path I have followed so far and the experiences that helped shape the direction I am taking now.

Emergency Services Background

My professional background is rooted in emergency services. Working in that field taught me how to think clearly in stressful situations, communicate effectively, and serve others in moments where reliability really matters.

Paramedic Experience

My experience as a paramedic helped strengthen my decision-making skills, work ethic, and ability to perform under pressure. It also taught me the importance of trust, preparation, and staying steady in fast-moving environments.

Transition Into IT

As I continued working in emergency services, I became more interested in technology, networking, and cybersecurity. That interest has turned into a serious career goal. I am now focused on learning the technical skills needed to build a future in the IT field.
