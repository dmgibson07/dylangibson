Career History

This page shares my career path that I have accomplished so far and the experiences that have helped shape the direction that I am taking now.

Emergency Services Background

My career history is rooted in the emergency service field. I have worked in this career field since 2010. I have worked as a Firefighter/Paramedic with various departments around the local area. I spent a large part of my career working full-time at Miami Valley Fire District, spending almost 15 years there. After making my decision to change career directions, I obtained a position with the Butler County Sheriff's Office as a paramedic, where I currently work. This has given me the opportunity to spend more time with my family and start my journey back into the IT career field. My experience as a Firefighter/Paramedic has helped strengthen my decision-making skills, work ethic, and ability to perform under pressure. It also taught me the importance of trust, preparation, and staying steady in fast-moving environments.

Transition Back Into IT

Starting my path back into the world of IT, I started going to school at Clark State. I decided to pursue a path into Cybersecurity. I currently major in Software Development and Cybersecurity. Before my emergency service career, I had spent my last two years of high school at a career center taking Information Technology classes. I also started off out of high school, going to school at Sinclair, majoring in Network Management before I had ever thought about getting into the fire service.
