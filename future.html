Future Goals

This page focuses on the direction I am heading and the goals I am working toward in the next stage of my career.

Short-Term Goals

For my short-term goals, I have begun to obtain IT certifications that will allow me to get my foot in the door in the IT field. I started with the basics and obtained my CompTIA A+ certification. Currently, while taking my college classes, I have been studying to obtain my Network+ certification. My goal is to find an entry-level job to fully transition into the world of IT.

Long-Term Goals

My long-term goal is to establish a strong career in the cybersecurity field. I like the idea of solving problems, protecting systems, and making a meaningful impact in the cyber world. This is the biggest reason why I chose Cybersecurity, as it almost felt like the IT version of being a Firefighter/Paramedic.

My Plan

My plan is to stay consistent, keep learning, and build experience one step at a time. I believe that discipline, patience, and effort will help me make a successful transition into the technology field.
